Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How do I book an appointment?

Book instantly with a live calendar on Bokadirekt, via our booking form, by phone 079-333 34 76 or email info@nouraskinsecrets.se.

Where is the salon located?

In central Uppsala at Kungsängsgatan 5B, 753 22 Uppsala — close to buses, trains and parking.

What are your opening hours?

We are open every day 10:00–20:00, by appointment only.

Is the salon really women only?

Yes. The salon is a safe, relaxed environment created exclusively for women.

How do I know which treatment suits me?

Try our skin assistant (chat button) which guides you based on your skin type. For a personal assessment we recommend a skin consultation (20 min, 450 kr).

How do I prepare for a treatment?

Come without makeup if possible, and avoid sun as well as strong acids/retinol a few days before most treatments. You always get personal instructions when booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, contact us as soon as possible by phone or email and we will help you find a new time.

Do the treatments hurt?

Most treatments are gentle and painless. For more intensive treatments such as microneedling, numbing cream is used. Let us know if you are sensitive and we will adapt the treatment.
